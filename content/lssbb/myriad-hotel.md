## Analyze
The dominant root causes on occupied residential/hospitality reroofs are disruption management, not roofing technique: noise and dust windows that have to be communicated to residents or guests in advance, interior water-intrusion risk during tear-off (a leak here is a life-safety/property-damage event, not just a punch-list item), and constrained staging/parking on an urban lot that competes with tenant or guest parking.

## Improve
A resident/guest notification protocol tied to the phased tear-off schedule, nightly dry-in verification before crews leave any section open, and a staging plan negotiated with property management before mobilization instead of worked out ad hoc on day one.
